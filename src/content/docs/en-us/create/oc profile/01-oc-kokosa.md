### Character Details

A hidden dark side (haraguro), appears to be the quiet type in public _(a literary girl?)_, but can be quite mean around friends, often saying things that spike people's blood pressure. Her train of thought is quite erratic and jumpy. She cares deeply about her online privacy and security, using an operating system similar to [OpenBSD](https://www.openbsd.org/).

_The world-building isn't fully written yet (), but anyway, it's an era with a strong hacker culture around the Y2K period._

Dislikes socializing; more socially lazy than socially anxious. A hardcore homebody (otaku girl) whose hobbies revolve around all things computer-related. Loves reading and photography, which offsets some of her otaku genes to the point where she will actually drag friends out to travel. Frequently uses a film camera.
